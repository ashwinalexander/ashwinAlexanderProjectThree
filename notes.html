Notes on Porsche prices

All 2020 Porsche pricing has been sourced from Porsche.com before adding on options and delivery costs. Used and classic Porshe prices are based on auction data from websites such as bringatrailer.com. Treat all used Porsche pricing as indicative. As with most collector cars, pricing varies based on a number of factors including mileage,provenance, accident history, and the fickleness of the North American collector car market.

About the Mercedes 500E

Eagle-eyed users of Porsche Picker might have encountered a Mercedes(!) in the results. The Mercedes 500E was assembled partly at the Porsche factory in Zuffenhausen and can be considered to be one of the first four door "Porsches", predating the Panamera of course. Back in the 90s, Porsche was going through difficult times and Mercedes contracted out assembly of the 500E to Porsche. Is the Mercedes 500E a Porsche? No. Was it built at the Porsche factory? Yes. And therefore, it finds a spot on this list.
